About us

Making way for a hearty meal is Omas Restaurant in Kannur. Established in the year 2017, this place is synonymous with delicious food that can satiate all food cravings. It is home to some of the most appreciated cuisines. So as to be able to cater to a large number of dinners, it occupies a favourable location at Kottayampoil. Courtesy to this strategic location, foodies in and around the neighborhood can walk in to this eating house conveniently without facing any hassles related to commuting to this part of the city.
